The Clash, formed in 1976, was a British Punk band from the UK. Considered one of the fore-fathers of modern day alternative music, The Clash was known not only for their trademark punk sound, but also exhibited very pronounced reggae, ska, and rockabilly styles as well. They were known for their rebellious political lyrics and themes. Their image was as rebellious as their sound sporting army jackets, combat boots, and mohawks.

The Clash successfully rocked the charts until they disbanded in 1986. Members all went on to other successful projects such as Big Audio Dynamite, The Pogues, Gorillaz, The Wallflowers, and many others.

This discography focuses solely on the original members of the Clash and on original studio albums and does not include live albums, compilations, or box sets.
